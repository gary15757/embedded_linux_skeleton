We're using qemu to test our rootfs. This way help to decrease the complex of test enviroment.

	sudo qemu-system-arm -nographic -kernel kernel-qemu-4.4.34-jessie -cpu arm1176 -m 256 -M versatilepb -append "console=ttyAMA0,115200  root=/dev/root ro loglevel=4" -initrd ../build/sdcard_boot/rootfs.cpio -net nic -net user,hostfwd=tcp::2022-:22,hostfwd=tcp::2080-:80

	ssh -p 2022 root@127.0.0.1 -o StrictHostKeyChecking=no -o UserKnownHostsFile=/dev/null
